About Huffman AI Solutions

We build AI-powered automation for home service businesses — so plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs, roofers, cleaners, landscapers, and handymen can stop drowning in office work and get back to the work they love.

Our Mission

Home service business owners are trapped. They became technicians because they're great at the work — but they're spending 15–25 hours a week answering phone calls, chasing leads, texting customers, sending invoices, and trying to keep up with marketing. They're working 12-hour days doing two jobs: theirs and an office manager's.

We built Huffman AI Solutions to fix that. We don't sell software and walk away. We build intelligent AI systems that actually do the work. They capture your leads, follow up automatically, book your appointments, send your invoices, chase your payments, post to your social media, request reviews, and answer your customers' questions. 24/7. In your voice. Without you lifting a finger.

We believe every home service business deserves the same automation power as big enterprises — without the enterprise price tag. That's our mission: empower home service business owners to grow and scale, unburdening them from the repetitive tasks that hold them back.
